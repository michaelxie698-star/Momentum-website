Important safety information
Meet Meresa ⌄

Meresa is a prefilled device that delivers a measured dose of semaglutide under your skin once a week. It is designed to be used at home after your clinician shows you how it works.

  • Used once weekly on the same day.
  • Available in multiple strengths so your dose can be adjusted gradually.
  • Intended to be used alongside a reduced‑calorie diet and increased activity.
About the ingredients ⌄

Each Meresa pen contains semaglutide, a GLP‑1 receptor agonist, along with inactive ingredients that keep the medication stable and ready to inject.

  • Active ingredient: semaglutide, a GLP‑1 receptor agonist used in other weight‑loss medications.
  • Inactive ingredients: stabilizers and sterile water for injection.
  • Tell your clinician about any allergies to medication ingredients before starting.
How to take Meresa ⌄

You inject Meresa once a week, with or without food, into the skin of your stomach, thigh, or upper arm, as directed by your clinician.

  • Choose a weekly injection day and stick with it.
  • Rotate injection sites to help reduce irritation.
  • If you miss a dose, follow your clinician’s instructions rather than taking extra doses.

Most people start on a lower dose that is increased slowly over time to help your body adjust.
