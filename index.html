How Maresa supports progress

GLP-1 medications like Maresa mimic hormones that help regulate appetite and blood sugar, allowing individuals to feel satiated and satisfied for longer periods of time when combined with changes in eating and activity.

With Fits, weekly Maresa injections are paired with clinician-guided dose adjustments, so your treatment can be slowed down or stepped up based on how you feel and how your body responds over time.

Maresa is meant to be one part of a broader plan: nutrition habits, movement you can stick with, sleep, and ongoing check-ins that help keep weight loss sustainable rather than a short-term sprint.

Maresa is taken once a week on the same day, with dose changes guided by your Fits clinician over time.
