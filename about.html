About FITS

Built for patients. Powered by FITS.

The company, the mission, and the model behind MARESA® access.

Company Overview

Healthcare access, designed with precision.

FITS is a modern distribution and support platform built to simplify customer access, product education, and order coordination for MARESA®.

FITS is an independent access, distribution, and information platform. FITS is not the manufacturer of MARESA®.

Mission

Make healthcare access simple.

We exist to remove friction from the path between a customer and the care they're seeking — through clear information, guided ordering, and dependable follow-up.

Vision

A platform that scales with trust.

As digital-first healthcare grows, we're building the infrastructure to support it — consistent, compliant, and built to serve more customers without losing quality.

Guided Access
Why FITS Exists

Access shouldn’t be the hard part.

Customers researching treatments like MARESA® often face fragmented information, unclear next steps, and inconsistent support. FITS was built to close that gap — one structured, guided experience from first question to ongoing care.

Consistent. Compliant. Built to scale.
